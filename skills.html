MY UX/UI SKILLS

“I love working in teams and learning from others. It makes the job more fun and fullfilling.”- Bea

User Research

Gaining an empathic understanding of the users through interviews and usability testing. This consits on creating a proto persona, running surveys, formulating interview questions, and conducting stakeholders and users interviews and tests. The main goal is to identify the users’ behavioral demographics, the traits or life experiences that influence their technology habits, and their frustrations and challenges, so we can define the their needs and expectations.

Problem Solving

Generating ideas, and challenging and validating solutions to target the users' needs and painpoints gathered through the user research. During this process we analyze the websites or interfaces for their use of design principles and heuristics. Common trends are identified using an affinity diagram and sorted into a prioritization matrix of the users' and stakeholders' wants and needs to know where to focus our efforts. We then move into card sorting and creating an inspiration board and a style guide that will direct our redesign.

User Interface Design

Executing the design solution from paper sketches to low-fidelity wireframes to high-fidelity prototypes applying UX/UI best practices. We conduct multiple rounds of usability testings during the all the design phases, from wireframes to high-fidelity prototypes, and interate on the design based on the testing feedback until they are at a place where they represent what the end product may look like. After the design has been tested and solidified, we move into the developing phase and apply the HTML, CSS, JQuery, JavaScript, Boostrap, and other necessary code in order publish the interface.
